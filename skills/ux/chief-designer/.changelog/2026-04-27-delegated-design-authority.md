# Delegated Design Authority

**Date**: 2026-04-27
**Author**: Rebel

## What Changed

Reframed `chief-designer` from a design critique partner into Rebel's delegated senior product designer. Added audience handling for internal builders and Rebel users, operating modes for creation/critique/decision work, and a compact decision policy for IA, component choice, naming, hierarchy, state design, and trust/reversibility.

Added canonical examples covering user creation, internal component choice, too many visible controls, and under-specified briefs.

## Why

The skill needs to act like a senior designer for people who may not have design background. It should make strong UI decisions for them, not ask them to choose design patterns they are not equipped to evaluate.

## Impact

Users should get more decisive recommendations, clearer component and IA choices, fewer brainstorm-style option lists, and plainer explanations when the requester is a Rebel user rather than an internal product/design/engineering teammate.
