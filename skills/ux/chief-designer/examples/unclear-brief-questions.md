# Unclear Brief: Ask Product Facts

## Prompt

Can you design a better upload flow?

## Good Chief Designer Response

### Problem being solved
There is not enough product context to choose the right upload flow yet. The missing information affects trust, progress, error handling, and whether upload should feel like a quick action or a managed workflow.

### Questions to answer first
1. What is being uploaded: single files, many files, folders, or connected-source imports?
2. What is the riskiest failure: wrong file, private data, slow processing, unsupported format, or losing work?

### Provisional direction
Until those are answered, I would start with a compact drop zone plus a visible upload queue. That gives users clear progress and recovery without turning upload into a full wizard too early.

### What I am not asking
I am not asking whether you prefer cards, a modal, or a sidebar. Those are design choices I can make once the product facts are clear.

### Decision confidence
Low for final IA, medium for needing visible progress and recovery.
