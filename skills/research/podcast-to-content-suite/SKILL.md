# Podcast to Content Suite

Transform a single podcast episode, webinar, or video transcript into multiple pieces of content, each tailored to its platform.

## [GOAL]

Maximise the value of audio/video content by extracting it into a complete, ready-to-publish content suite.

## [PROCESS]

1. **Intake**
   - Get the transcript (file path, paste, or URL for YouTube transcripts)
   - Ask: "Which outputs do you need? All of them, or specific ones?"
     - Show notes with timestamps
     - Blog post / article
     - LinkedIn post
     - Twitter/X thread
     - Newsletter section
     - Key quotes and soundbites
   - Ask: "What's the tone? Professional, conversational, thought-leadership?"
   - Ask: "Who's the audience for this content?"

2. **Analyse the transcript**
   - Identify the 3-5 key themes or insights
   - Extract the best quotes (punchy, standalone, attributable)
   - Note timestamps for major topic transitions
   - Identify the most compelling stories or examples
   - Find the "headline insight" — the single most shareable takeaway

3. **Generate the suite**

   **Show Notes** (if requested):
   - Episode title and one-line description
   - Guest introduction (if applicable)
   - Timestamped topic outline (HH:MM — Topic)
   - Key takeaways (3-5 bullets)
   - Links and resources mentioned

   **Blog Post** (if requested):
   - Engaging headline derived from the headline insight
   - 800-1200 word article that restructures the conversation into a narrative
   - Not a transcript summary — a standalone piece that works for someone who never listens
   - Include 2-3 direct quotes from the conversation
   - Clear conclusion and call-to-action

   **LinkedIn Post** (if requested):
   - Hook (first line that stops the scroll)
   - One key insight, expanded with context
   - Personal angle or takeaway
   - 150-300 words
   - End with a question or call to engage

   **Twitter/X Thread** (if requested):
   - 5-8 tweets that tell a coherent story
   - Tweet 1: hook with the headline insight
   - Body tweets: one idea per tweet, each standalone-readable
   - Final tweet: summary + link to full episode

   **Newsletter Section** (if requested):
   - 2-3 paragraph summary positioned as "what I learned this week"
   - One standout quote
   - Link to full episode

   **Key Quotes** (always generated):
   - 5-10 standalone quotes, formatted for sharing
   - Each with speaker attribution and brief context

4. **Review**
   - Present the suite to the user
   - Ask: "Anything to adjust in tone, emphasis, or length?"

## [IMPORTANT]

- Each piece of content should work independently. Don't assume the reader listened to the episode.
- Match the user's voice and brand, not the podcast guest's voice.
- Attribute quotes properly. Never present a guest's words as the user's own.
- If the transcript quality is poor, note that and do the best you can with what's there.
- Don't over-produce. The user can edit — give them a strong draft, not a polished essay.
