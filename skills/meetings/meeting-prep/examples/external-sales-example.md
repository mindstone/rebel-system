## Key Questions to Ask

**Qualifying questions:**
- What's the business problem you're trying to solve? (Get specific metrics - e.g., "reduce stockouts by X%")
- What's driving this project right now? Why not 6 months ago or 6 months from now?
- Who else is involved in the decision beyond you two?
- What does success look like 6 months after implementing a solution?
- What's your evaluation process and timeline?

**Technical discovery:**
- What's your current data stack? (cloud data warehouse, BI tools, etc.)
- How many users would need access? (helps with pricing)
- What's your biggest pain point with current analytics approach?
- Have you built custom forecasting models internally, or using off-the-shelf?

**Competition & budget:**
- Are you evaluating other vendors? Which ones?
- What's your budget range for this project?
- Who controls budget approval for a project like this?
