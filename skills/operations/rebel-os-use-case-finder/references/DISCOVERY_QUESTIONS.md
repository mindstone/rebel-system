# Discovery Questions

Few-shot examples of coaching-style questions to surface high-impact use cases. Use your judgment — pick or adapt 2-3 that fit what you already know about the user. These are inspiration, not a script.

If you already have strong signal from memory and connectors, skip this and go straight to research.

## Pain Discovery

- "What's one task you regularly put off or dread doing?"
- "What takes you 30+ minutes that you wish took 5?"
- "What's the most tedious recurring part of your week?"

## Automation Candidates

- "What do you do every week that follows roughly the same steps?"
- "If something could run overnight and have results waiting for you in the morning, what would it be?"
- "What information do you always wish you had before a meeting — but rarely have time to pull together?"

## Hidden Value

- "What would you do more of if you had an extra 2 hours a week?"
- "What's something you know you *should* be doing regularly but never get to?"

## Usage Notes

- Ask **at most 3** — don't turn this into a long interview
- Pick based on what you already know: lots of meetings → lean toward prep questions; admin-heavy → lean toward automation candidates
- The answers should directly inform which connectors to research and what patterns to look for
