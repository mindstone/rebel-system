# Strategic AI Partnership Proposal
## Acme Training × TechVentures Capital
### Building the Regional AI Innovation Ecosystem Together

---

## Partnership Vision

Following successful AI training programmes across TechVentures' portfolio companies, this partnership takes us to the next level - positioning the entire portfolio at the forefront of AI innovation while establishing the region as a leading AI hub.

This isn't just about training anymore. It's about creating a sustained competitive advantage for your portfolio while simultaneously elevating the region's position in the global AI landscape. Together, we can build an ecosystem that attracts international attention, investment, and talent, with TechVentures at the centre of this transformation.

---

## What TechVentures Receives

### 1. Quarterly Strategic Days (4x per year)
- **Full day on-site** with our CEO personally
- **Morning**: Portfolio-wide strategic session on latest AI developments
- **Afternoon**: Deep-dive working sessions with specific portfolio companies
- **Executive AI Briefing**: One-hour "suspension of disbelief" session open to executives from across the ecosystem
- **Evening**: Community engagement (when aligned with meetups/events)

### 2. Monthly Office Hours (10x per year)
- **Virtual Q&A sessions** providing a regular drumbeat for the portfolio
- **Open forum**: Teams bring their specific AI challenges and questions
- **Brief AI updates** on latest developments, then focused on answering real implementation questions
- **Open to all teams** across TechVentures ecosystem
- **Recorded for those who can't attend live**

### 3. AI Ecosystem Leadership Support
- **Monthly AI Meetup backing**: Content support, speaker connections, format guidance
- **Continuous learning**: First access to new insights and capabilities
- **Annual "State of AI in Regional Startups" Report**: Joint research and publication positioning the region (and TechVentures) as innovation leaders
- **Case Study Development**: Document portfolio success stories with PR amplification
- **Strategic introductions**: Connections to global AI community and potential partners

## What We Build Together

These ecosystem initiatives create value for the broader startup community while strengthening TechVentures' position as the innovation hub:

### Quarterly Entrepreneur Cohorts
- **Target**: Regional startup founders and early employees needing AI upskilling
- **Format**: Training programme adapted for local market
- **Revenue share**: 50/50 split on profits
- **TechVentures value**: Strengthens portfolio and attracts new ventures

### AI Innovation Weekends (2x per year)
- **Target**: High-level executives and founders
- **Format**: Intensive weekend workshops
- **Revenue share**: To be determined based on contribution
- **TechVentures value**: Positions you as the innovation hub in the region

### Community Events
- **Monthly meetups**: Already committed and ongoing
- **Special events**: Larger conferences or summits
- **Sponsorship opportunities**: For TechVentures portfolio companies
- **TechVentures value**: Talent attraction and ecosystem leadership
