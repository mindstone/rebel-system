# Enterprise AI Academy Proposal
## GlobalMedia Corp - Board Session + Senior Leadership Pilot

---

## Executive Summary

GlobalMedia Corp has achieved 57% AI literacy across your organization—a remarkable accomplishment—but the strategic governance and cultural transformation required to turn AI adoption into competitive advantage starts at board level. Your recent discussion revealed the gap: teams using AI in silos without strategic coordination, and leadership uncertain about governance frameworks.

This proposal outlines a two-stage pilot starting with your 8-person board in a 4-hour "suspension of disbelief" session, immediately followed by comprehensive enablement for up to 150 senior leaders.

---

## The Challenge

**Board-Level Governance Gap**
"We don't have a unified AI strategy yet" — creating cohesive AI governance requires shared language and experience at the highest level.

**Cultural Divide**
Over half of employees hide their AI usage from managers. Editorial teams embrace AI while commercial teams lag behind. This fragmentation prevents systematic value capture.

**Strategic Decision-Making**
People use AI as "thought partner" for research but not for actual decision-making at scale. The gap between experimentation and strategic deployment remains wide.

---

## The Solution: Two-Stage Approach

### Stage 1: Board "Suspension of Disbelief" Session

**Participants:** 8-person board  
**Format:** 4-hour in-person session at your headquarters  
**Delivery:** CEO personally leading  
**Structure:** 2 hours live demonstration + 2 hours hands-on application  
**Investment:** $25,000

**What This Delivers:**

During the first 2 hours, we'll work through real GlobalMedia board-level challenges live—whether that's content strategy optimization, audience analytics, or competitive positioning. You mentioned these as active board considerations; we'll tackle one using 6-7 different AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Deep Research, Gamma, NotebookLM) in real-time to show what's possible today.

**Expected Outcomes:**
- 2X increase in board members' understanding of immediate AI applications
- 4X increase in perceived 2-year AI opportunity
- Consensus on organisation-wide AI enablement approach
- Clear signal to senior management that this matters

**Why This Matters First:**

We refuse engagements that don't start with executives—not because of principle, but because we've learned it doesn't work otherwise. When your board can demonstrate AI's value for strategic decisions, adoption becomes non-negotiable throughout the organisation.

---

### Stage 2: Senior Leadership Pilot Programme (150 People)

**Format:** 10-hour programme over 4 weeks (delivered remotely)  
**Structure:** 4 × 1-hour live demos + 6 hours personalised platform content  
**Delivery:** Customised by function (Editorial, Commercial, Operations, Finance)

**What This Includes:**
- **Personalised Learning Journeys**: Platform customises all examples to individual roles
- **Safe Sandbox Environment**: Practice with feedback before using with real work
- **AI Coach Browser Extension**: Real-time prompting feedback in ChatGPT/Gemini/Claude
- **Evidence Points Gamification**: Tracks practical application
- **Multi-LLM Training**: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude
- **12-Month Platform Access**: Ongoing content updates, customer success support
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track adoption, usage patterns, productivity gains

**Programme Focus by Role:**

**For the CEO/Board:**
- Strategic scenario planning with AI-augmented analysis
- Competitive intelligence synthesis across multiple sources
- Board deck preparation and presentation enhancement

**For Editorial Leaders:**
- Content strategy optimization and audience analysis
- Research synthesis and fact-checking workflows
- Headline testing and engagement prediction

**For Commercial Leaders:**
- Client proposal personalization at scale
- Market analysis and opportunity identification
- Sales enablement and pitch deck creation

**Programme Delivers:**

On average, participants reclaim 5.5 hours per week by programme completion—meaning they save more time than the programme takes. This isn't from basic tool use but from getting the details right: reverse prompting ("ask me one question at a time"), chain of thought reasoning, and the mindset shift of thinking differently about work.
