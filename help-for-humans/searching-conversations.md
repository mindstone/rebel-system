# Searching Conversations

Can't remember which conversation had that great insight? Rebel makes it easy to find past conversations by what they were about, not just when they happened.

## Quick Search

Click the **search icon** in the sidebar (or use the search field at the top of your conversation history) to search across all your past conversations.

### Search by Topic

Just type what you're looking for:
- "budget planning"
- "meeting with Sarah"
- "that email about the proposal"

Rebel combines keyword matching and semantic search — it finds exact words *and* understands meaning. So "Q3 projections" finds conversations containing those words, but also ones where you discussed "third quarter forecasts." You get the precision of keywords with the intelligence of meaning-based search.

Exact title and keyword matches are no longer filtered out just because their meaning-match score looks low — once search is ready and within your active filters, the word you remember should surface the conversation. Short names and acronyms return matches instantly, and if you rename a conversation, it's findable by its new name straight away.

### Deep Search

Search now covers all your messages in each conversation (not just the first one), plus the first assistant response — so mid-conversation insights are just as findable as the opening question. Results scan up to 200 matches, ranked by relevance, and are deduplicated so you won't see the same conversation listed more than once.

### Understanding Search Sections

You may notice **"Also relevant"** and **"Search all messages"** sections in your results. Hover over each heading to see a tooltip explaining what it shows — "Also relevant" surfaces conversations that are topically related, while "Search all messages" does a broader scan across all message content.

### Search by Time

Use the **recency filters** to narrow the search itself:
- **1d** — Conversations from the past 24 hours
- **7d** — Past 7 days
- **30d** — Past 30 days
- **All time** — Everything

The time filter narrows the actual search, not just the handful of results shown — so a time-boxed search surfaces far more of what's genuinely in that window. Need a broader pass? **Search all messages** scans deeper across the whole window (up to the result cap). Recent conversations are boosted in results too, so your latest work appears first even when searching broadly.

Automation runs are searchable as well — switch the history filter to **Automations** to search across them.

### Direct Navigation

If you have a conversation ID or a `rebel://` URL (from a shared link, notification, or diagnostic log), paste it directly into the search bar. Rebel recognizes the ID format and navigates straight to that conversation.

## When search can't run

Sometimes Rebel says it's still getting search ready, or that search is briefly unavailable, instead of showing results. Rebel now tells these two apart so "no results" only ever means *no results*:

- **"Getting search ready…"** — the search index is still warming up. It resolves itself in a moment; no action needed. Your conversations aren't gone — search just isn't ready yet.
- **"Search is taking a breather"** (with a **Try again** button) — search hit a temporary snag. Your conversations are safe; click Try again, or wait a moment and search again.

If you see a normal empty result with no warning, Rebel searched and genuinely found no matches. Try different keywords, a wider time range, or **Find Similar** from a conversation you do remember.

## Tips for Finding Conversations

- **Be specific** — "marketing budget review" works better than just "budget"
- **Use names** — "conversation with Jamie" helps narrow things down
- **Try different phrases** — If one search doesn't find it, try describing it differently

## Find Similar

Right-click any conversation in the sidebar and select **Find Similar** to discover related conversations. Rebel searches across all your past conversations for ones that cover similar topics — useful when you remember working on something related but can't recall the details.


## Starred conversations

For conversations you know you'll want to find again:
- **Star important conversations** — Click the star icon to add to Starred
- **Starred conversations appear at the top** — Easy access without searching
