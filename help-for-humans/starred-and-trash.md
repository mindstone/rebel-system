# Starred and Trash

Organize your conversations with stars, **Done**, and Trash. Your starred conversations stay easy to reach, and deleted conversations get a second chance before they are gone for good.

## See Also

- [getting-started.md](getting-started.md) — First steps with Rebel
- [terminology.md](terminology.md) — Definitions of key concepts

## Starred

Star conversations you want to find quickly. Starred conversations float to the top of your sidebar, no scrolling expedition required.

### Starring a conversation

Two ways to add a conversation to Starred:

1. **Star icon** — Click the star icon on any conversation in the sidebar or conversation header
2. **Conversation menu** — Open the menu (three dots) on any conversation and select **Star**

### Unstarring

Changed your mind? Click the star again or use the menu to remove it from Starred. The conversation moves back to its normal position in your history.

### Why use Starred

- Keep ongoing projects within easy reach
- Keep important reference conversations at hand
- Mark conversations you want to return to later

Stars are personal — they only affect how *you* see your conversation list.

## Trash

When you delete a conversation, it moves to Trash instead of vanishing immediately. Think of it as a safety net.

### Deleting conversations

Click the delete option in the conversation menu. The conversation moves to Trash, where:

- it will not appear in search results
- it is excluded from **Ctrl+Tab** navigation
- it is hidden from your main conversation list

**Note:** If a conversation is currently running, Rebel will ask you to stop it first before moving it to Trash.

### Restoring from Trash

Changed your mind? Open Trash from the sidebar and restore the conversation. It returns to your history exactly as it was.

### Emptying Trash

To permanently delete conversations:

1. Expand the Trash section in the sidebar
2. Click the **Empty** button inside the expanded Trash section
3. Confirm the deletion

**This cannot be undone.** Once emptied, those conversations are gone for good.

## Sidebar sections

Your conversation history is organised into sections:

| Section | What's there |
|---|---|
| **Starred** | Starred conversations (always at top) |
| **Active** | Recent and ongoing conversations you're working on (not automation runs) |
| **Done** | Older conversations you have finished or explicitly marked as done |
| **Trash** | Deleted conversations awaiting permanent removal |

Each section has its own icon so you can quickly tell where a conversation lives.

### Active vs Done

Conversations naturally move from Active to Done based on age and activity. You can also move one there yourself when it is finished and you want it out of the way without deleting it.

To do that: use the conversation menu and select **Mark as done**.

To bring it back: find it in **Done** and select **Reopen**.

**Automation runs aren't Active.** Scheduled or event-triggered runs stay out of **Active** — and out of pinned tabs, unread counts, and the "active" lists on Homepage and mobile — so your sidebar reflects *your* conversations, not background jobs Rebel kicked off on its own. Open them from [Automations](rebel://automations) run history, or find them in the **All** tab. They can still appear in **Starred** or **Done** if you put them there; they just won't masquerade as Active while they run.

## Quick reference

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| Star a conversation | Click the star icon or use the menu |
| Unstar | Click the star again |
| Delete | Conversation menu → Delete |
| Restore from Trash | Open Trash → Restore |
| Permanently delete | Empty Trash |
| Mark as done | Conversation menu → Mark as done |
| Reopen | Open Done → Reopen |
