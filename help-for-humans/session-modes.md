# Session Modes

Most people only need four ideas here:

- **Conversations**
- **Automations**
- **Privacy Mode**
- **Voice Mode**

That's the language you'll actually see in the app.

## What you see in the app

| Label you see | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Conversations** | Your normal chats with Rebel |
| **Automations** | Scheduled or event-triggered runs Rebel handles in the background |
| **Privacy Mode** | Extra approval and caution for sensitive work |
| **Voice Mode** | Shorter, more spoken-style replies while you're talking to Rebel |

In the history filter, for example, the choices are **All**, **Conversations**, and **Automations**. Automation runs stay out of your **Active** conversation list — they won't crowd pinned tabs, unread counts, or Homepage/mobile "active" surfaces. Open them from [Automations](rebel://automations) run history, or browse **All** if you want everything in one place.

## Conversations

This is normal day-to-day Rebel use:

- you type or speak to Rebel
- Rebel can ask follow-up questions if needed
- Rebel works in front of you, not off on its own

If you do nothing special, this is where you are.

## Automations

Automations are background runs that happen on a schedule or when something specific happens, such as transcripts arriving.

When an automation runs:

- Rebel keeps going without waiting for you
- Rebel makes reasonable assumptions instead of asking clarifying questions
- Rebel still follows your safety rules
- Rebel still pauses if something genuinely needs approval

So: more independent, not less safe.

They also stay out of **Active** on purpose — your sidebar is for conversations you're in, not every background job Rebel runs on a schedule. Find automation runs in [Automations](rebel://automations), or in the **All** history filter.

See [Automations](library://rebel-system/help-for-humans/automations.md) for the full guide.

## Privacy Mode

Privacy Mode is a **toggle**, not a separate kind of conversation.

When it's on, Rebel is more cautious about:

- tool use
- saving information
- actions that persist data

See [Privacy Mode](library://rebel-system/help-for-humans/privacy-mode.md).

## Voice Mode

Voice Mode is also a **toggle**, not a separate session type.

When voice is active, Rebel usually responds more like a capable colleague speaking aloud:

- shorter
- clearer
- less formatting-heavy

See [Voice and Audio](library://rebel-system/help-for-humans/voice-and-audio.md).

## If you see “Interactive” anywhere

You may occasionally see **Interactive** in diagnostics or technical surfaces.

That simply means a normal **Conversation**.

So the plain-English mapping is:

- **Interactive** = **Conversation**
- **Automation** = **Automation**

That is the main translation. No decoder ring required.
