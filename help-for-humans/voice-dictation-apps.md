# Voice dictation apps

> **Looking for Rebel's built-in voice features?** See [voice-and-audio](library://rebel-system/help-for-humans/voice-and-audio.md) for the full guide to talking with Rebel.

Talking is a huge unlock when working with AI.

Voice is better than typing, because it's faster and lower cognitive effort. And people tend to ramble a bit when talking, which usually means they provide more context (very useful).

But usually reading > listening, because eyes are faster than ears, and reading makes it easier to digest, skim, and jump around.

And if the conversation turns out useful, copy it as Markdown from the conversation menu. Civilised, really.


## Built-in Voice in Rebel

Rebel has built-in voice features: press the microphone button to talk, or use the global hotkey (Ctrl+Alt+Space) for hands-free interaction. Rebel can also speak responses aloud.

**Providers:** Local **Moonshine** is the newer on-device option and the desktop default. Local **Parakeet** is the older desktop-only local alternative. If you want cloud transcription instead, use **OpenAI Whisper** for maximum accuracy or **ElevenLabs Scribe** for faster transcription.

See [voice-and-audio](library://rebel-system/help-for-humans/voice-and-audio.md) for the complete setup guide, custom vocabulary, and troubleshooting.


## External dictation apps

- Josh likes [Monologue](https://www.monologue.to/)
- Greg likes [Aqua](https://aquavoice.com/)
- [VoiceInk](https://tryvoiceink.com/) has the benefit of being private (runs entirely on your computer)
- Other people like [Wispr Flow](https://wisprflow.ai/), and [Superwhisper](https://superwhisper.com/).

(Most of these lean Mac-first. If you're on Windows, Rebel's built-in voice is usually the simpler bet.)


## Advanced tips

- Next time you're trying to work through something or provide instructions for a nuanced task, ask the AI to interview you, one or two questions at a time.
  - You can even try putting on headphones, and giving long discursive answers while folding the laundry.
