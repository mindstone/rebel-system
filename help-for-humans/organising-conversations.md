# Organising your conversations

The conversation sidebar can hold a lot of history. Two tools keep it tidy: **folders** (group related chats) and **marking conversations done** (clear finished work out of the way without deleting it). Nothing here ever throws a conversation away — done and foldered chats stay fully usable.

## The sidebar tabs

Filter chips at the top of the sidebar switch what you're looking at:

- **Active** — conversations you're still working on. This is the default view. Scheduled, event-triggered, and other background automation runs never appear here — those live under [Automations](rebel://automations).
- **Starred** (the star) — conversations you've starred for quick access, kept separate from everything else.
- **Done** — conversations you've finished. They're out of Active but still here whenever you want them.
- **All** — everything in one list.

## Marking a conversation done

When you've finished with a conversation, right-click it in the sidebar (or use its row actions) and choose **Mark as done**. It leaves the **Active** tab and moves to **Done** — no deleting, no losing it.

Changed your mind? Open the **Done** tab, find it, and choose **Reopen**. It comes straight back to Active. Done conversations stay completely usable in the meantime: open, rename, star, or delete them like any other.

## Folders

Folders group related conversations — a project, a client, a recurring topic.

- **Add a conversation to a folder** — right-click it and choose **Move to folder…**, then either pick an existing folder or type a name to create a new one. You can also **drag** a conversation onto a folder.
- **Take one out** — **Remove from folder** puts it back in the ungrouped list.
- **Rename or delete a folder** — use the actions on the folder's header row. Deleting a folder doesn't delete its conversations; they return to the ungrouped list.

If you create a great many folders, Rebel will gently suggest broader categories — folders are most useful when there aren't too many to scan.

### Finished work stays tucked in its folder

Mark a conversation done while it's in a folder and it doesn't vanish to the **Done** tab on its own. Instead, it slips into a collapsed **Done (N)** group **inside that folder**, right beneath the folder's active conversations on the **Active** tab. So the folder stays self-contained — you can still see (and reopen) what you finished without leaving Active.

- The **Done (N)** group is collapsed by default. Click to expand and peek at the finished conversations; click again to hide them. Rebel remembers your choice for each folder.
- Finished conversations in the group are shown muted but work fully — open, rename, star, reopen, or delete.
- Open a link to a conversation that's done-and-in-a-folder and Rebel automatically expands its folder and the Done group so you land right on it.
- When **every** conversation in a folder is done, the whole folder moves to the **Done** tab, as you'd expect.

The **Done** tab keeps the same folder grouping, showing each folder's finished conversations.

## A note on syncing

Your folders and their conversations travel with you — they're included when you move Rebel to a new computer or use it across devices, so your organisation isn't left behind.
