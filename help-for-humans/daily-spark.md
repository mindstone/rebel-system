# Daily Spark

Daily Spark is a small personal note that can appear on Home each morning.

It is not a motivational poster. It is Rebel noticing the shape of your week and offering one short aside with enough restraint to remain employable.

---

## What it does

Each week, Rebel locally prepares seven short literary asides shaped by your recent work pattern:

- calendar density
- project trajectory
- meeting patterns
- whether the week looks light, heavy, or best left undisturbed

The format can vary: limerick, haiku, telegram, sommelier note, dry one-liner, and other small forms that know when to leave.

One note reveals each day. Heavy weeks automatically shift toward softer formats. If the week looks severely distressing, Daily Spark stays silent rather than risk saying something glib at precisely the wrong moment.

---

## Privacy

Daily Spark is generated locally, and the text never leaves your desktop.

It uses the contour of your week, not a cloud horoscope. Small mercies.

---

## How to use it

Open **Home** in the morning. If Daily Spark has something appropriate for the day, it appears there.

There is nothing to run manually. The point is a small note, not another workflow wearing a hat.

---

## How to turn it off

Go to **Settings → Agents → Personalisation** and turn off **Daily Spark**.

You can turn it back on from the same place if you miss being gently heckled by your calendar.
