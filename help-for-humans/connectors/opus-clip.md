# OpusClip (Official)

Turn long-form videos into short, social-ready clips — clip, caption, reframe, censor, and post, straight from a chat message.


## What You Can Do

- **Clip a YouTube or Vimeo link** into vertical shorts ("clip this keynote into five shorts")
- **Upload a local video** for clipping
- **Fetch transcripts and editing scripts**, edit clips and re-render
- **Organize clips** into collections and export HD downloads
- **Post or schedule** clips to your connected social accounts


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **OpusClip (Official)** and click **Connect**
3. Sign in with your OpusClip account in the browser window that opens

That's it — no API key to copy.

**Plan note:** signing in works on any OpusClip account, but actually *using* the tools requires a Pro Beta, Max, or Business plan. Without one, the connector will politely suggest upgrading.


## Troubleshooting

- **Sign-in fails with "No matching client"** — use the same sign-in method (email, Google, or Apple) you originally used for OpusClip.
- **"API access not enabled for this org"** — your OpusClip organization doesn't have API access; Pro Beta and Max plans include it automatically.
- **Errors about limits** — monthly credit caps reset on the 1st (UTC); at most 4 clipping projects can run at once, so let one finish before starting another.
- **Repeated sign-in prompts** — remove the connection and add it again to refresh the token.


## Tips

- Clip previews appear right away; ask Rebel to *export* a clip when you want the HD download link.
- Clipping and re-rendering use OpusClip credits — listing and reading things doesn't.
