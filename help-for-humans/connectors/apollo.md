# Apollo

Sales intelligence and prospecting: search 200M+ B2B contacts and companies, enrich people and accounts with verified emails and phone numbers, and enroll prospects into outreach sequences.


## What You Can Do

- **Find** people and companies that match your ideal customer profile
- **Enrich** contacts and accounts with verified emails and phone numbers
- **Save** contacts and companies to your Apollo CRM
- **Enroll** prospects into outreach sequences
- **Track** sequence and engagement performance


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **Apollo** and click **Set up with Rebel**
3. Sign in to your Apollo account
4. Authorize Rebel to access your Apollo data

No API key needed — Apollo handles sign-in through your browser.


## Tips

- **Targeted prospecting**: "Find VPs of Marketing at Series B SaaS companies in the US"
- **Enrich and engage**: "Enrich this contact, then add them to my outbound sequence"
- **Heads up on credits**: actions that spend Apollo credits (enrichment, sequence enrollment) ask for your approval first.
