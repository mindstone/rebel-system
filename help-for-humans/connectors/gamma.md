# Gamma

Generate presentations with AI: create slide decks from prompts, apply themes, and export to PDF or PowerPoint.


## What You Can Do

- **Generate presentations** from text prompts
- **Apply themes** to customize the look
- **Check status** of generation tasks
- **Export** to PDF or PowerPoint


## Setup

Gamma requires an API key:

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **Gamma** and click **Set up with Rebel**
3. Click **Open Gamma** to get your API key, then paste it


## Alternatives

Other presentation tools with MCP support:

- [Google Slides MCP](https://github.com/matteoantoci/google-slides-mcp) — Create/edit Google Slides
- [PowerPoint MCP](https://github.com/GongRzhe/Office-PowerPoint-MCP-Server) — Local PPTX creation
- [SlideSpeak MCP](https://github.com/SlideSpeak/slidespeak-mcp) — AI presentation generation
- [Canva MCP](https://www.canva.dev/docs/connect/canva-mcp-server-setup/) — Design and presentations

Gamma is geared toward web-native presentations with built-in sharing and analytics. Choose based on your preferred output format and workflow.
