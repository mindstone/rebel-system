# Mailchimp

Access your Mailchimp email marketing data: browse campaigns, check audience stats, view subscriber lists, read templates, and analyse campaign performance.


## What You Can Do

- **View campaigns**: See all your email campaigns and their details
- **Check audiences**: Browse your lists, subscriber counts, and segments
- **Read reports**: Get open rates, click rates, and campaign performance metrics
- **View templates**: Browse your email templates
- **See automations**: Check classic automation workflows and their emails
- **E-commerce data**: View connected store data, products, and orders

> **Read-only access**: Rebel can view your Mailchimp data but cannot create campaigns, add subscribers, or modify anything.


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **Mailchimp** and click **Set up**
3. Click **Open Mailchimp** to go to your API key settings
4. Click **Create A Key**, name it (e.g., "Rebel"), and click **Generate Key**
5. Copy the key immediately -- it won't be shown again
6. Paste the key back in Rebel

> **API key format**: Your key includes a data center suffix (e.g., `xxxxxxxx-us1`). Copy the entire key including this suffix.


## Tips

- **Campaign performance**: "What's the open rate on my last campaign?" -- Rebel reads the campaign report
- **Audience health**: "How many subscribers are on my main list?" -- checks list stats
- **Browse templates**: "Show my email templates" -- lists available templates
- **Campaign history**: "Show my campaigns from the last month" -- lists recent sends
