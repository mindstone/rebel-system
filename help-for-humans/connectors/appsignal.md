# AppSignal

Access your AppSignal monitoring data: track application performance, review error trends, and stay on top of incidents — all from within Rebel.


## What You Can Do

- **Monitor performance** — check response times, throughput, and queue metrics
- **Track errors** — review error occurrences, frequency, and affected endpoints
- **Review incidents** — see open incidents and their status


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors** in Rebel
2. Find **AppSignal** and click **Set up**
3. Follow the authentication flow to connect your AppSignal account

- "Show me recent errors in production"
- "What's the performance trend for the API this week?"
