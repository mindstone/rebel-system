# Linear

Track issues and projects: search, create, and update issues, manage projects, and add comments.


## What You Can Do

- **Search** issues and projects
- **Create** new issues with title, description, and assignee
- **Update** issue status, priority, and labels
- **Comment** on issues
- **View** project progress and team workload


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **Linear** and click **Set up with Rebel**
3. Sign in to your Linear workspace
4. Authorize Rebel to access your issues


## Tips

- **Quick issue creation**: "Create a Linear issue for fixing the login bug, assign to me"
- **Status updates**: "Move all my in-progress issues to done"
