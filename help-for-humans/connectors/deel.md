# Deel

Access your Deel HR and payroll platform: manage contracts, view people and employees, check invoices and payments, handle time off requests, and track expenses and timesheets.


## What You Can Do

- **Contracts**: View and manage contractor and employee contracts
- **People**: Browse employees and contractors across regions
- **Invoices & payments**: Check pending and paid invoices
- **Time off**: View and manage time off requests
- **Expenses & timesheets**: Track expenses and submitted timesheets


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **Deel** and click **Set up**
3. Sign in to Deel in the browser window that opens
4. Review the permissions requested
5. Click **Allow** to grant access

> **Note**: Access matches your Deel account permissions. Org Admins have full access; other roles may see limited data.


## Tips

- **Team overview**: "Who are our contractors in EMEA?" or "Show all active employees"
- **Invoices**: "Show pending invoices" or "What payments are due this month?"
- **Time off**: "Check time off for the team this week"
- **Contracts**: "Show expiring contracts in the next 30 days"
