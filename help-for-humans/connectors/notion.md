# Notion

Search and read your Notion workspace, access pages and databases, and manage content directly from Rebel.


## What You Can Do

- **Search** across all your Notion pages and databases
- **Read** page content, comments, and database entries
- **Create** new pages and update existing ones
- **Manage** database entries and comments


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **Notion** and click **Set up with Rebel**
3. Sign in to your Notion workspace
4. Select which pages/databases to share with Rebel

You can grant access to your entire workspace or specific pages only.


## Tips

- **Page access**: Notion asks which pages to share during setup. You can modify access later in Notion's settings
- **Database queries**: Ask Rebel to search or filter database entries using natural language


## Local Sync Alternative

For offline access or version-controlled workflows, you can sync Notion pages to local Markdown files. See [Notion-access-and-syncing](library://rebel-system/help-for-humans/Notion-access-and-syncing.md) for details.
