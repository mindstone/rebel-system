# HubSpot

Access your full HubSpot CRM: search and manage contacts, companies, deals, tickets, products, forms, and conversations. Log calls and meetings. View marketing email stats and website analytics. Create and manage Knowledge Base articles.


## What You Can Do

**CRM Data**
- **Search** contacts, companies, deals, tickets, tasks, products, and line items
- **Read** detailed records, associations, and engagement history
- **Create** and **update** records across all object types
- **Log** calls and meetings linked to contacts and deals
- **Trace** line items back to their deals, so revenue details don't wander off unsupervised

**Conversations Inbox**
- **Read** HubSpot Conversations Inbox threads
- **Review** customer messages alongside the CRM records they relate to

**Knowledge Base (requires Service Hub Professional or Enterprise)**
- **Browse** your Knowledge Bases to see what's published and what's in draft
- **Search** articles by keyword or phrase
- **Read** the full content of any article

**Marketing (requires Marketing Hub)**
- **Forms**: View form configurations and recent submissions
- **Analytics**: Get website traffic reports by source, location, or campaign
- **Email stats**: Review marketing email performance (opens, clicks, bounces)

**Sales Operations**
- **Products**: Manage your product catalog (name, SKU, pricing)
- **Line items**: Add products to deals and trace existing line items back to the deals they belong to
- **Pipelines**: View deal and ticket pipeline stages
- **Auto-attribution**: Records created through Rebel are automatically tagged with Rebel attribution and the deal owner


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **HubSpot** and click **Set up with Rebel**
3. Sign in to your HubSpot account
4. Authorize Rebel to access your CRM

> **Free HubSpot accounts**: Rebel automatically enables read-only mode for free accounts. To create or update records, upgrade to a paid HubSpot plan.

> **Marketing features**: Analytics and marketing email tools require Marketing Hub Professional or Enterprise. They'll show a clear error on accounts without this subscription.

> **Knowledge Base features**: Knowledge Base tools require Service Hub Professional or Enterprise. If your account doesn't include this, you'll see a clear message explaining what's needed.


## Tips

- **Natural language search**: Ask "find contacts at Acme Corp" or "show deals closing this month"
- **Add products to deals**: "Add the Enterprise License to the Acme deal" — creates a line item linked to the deal. You can also ask which deal an existing line item belongs to
- **Conversations Inbox**: "Show recent customer conversations about billing" or "Summarise this week's support inbox themes"
- **Form submissions**: "Show me the last 10 submissions for our Contact Us form"
- **Call logging**: "Log a 15-minute call with John about the proposal"
- **Knowledge Base**: "Show me all draft KB articles" or "Find Knowledge Base articles about account setup"


## Troubleshooting

- If HubSpot denies access to a capability (**support tickets** is the common one), reconnecting won't add it — a HubSpot administrator needs to enable that access, or your HubSpot plan may need to include it. Everything else stays connected and working.
