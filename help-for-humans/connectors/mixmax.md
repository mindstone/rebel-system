# Mixmax

Send tracked emails, manage sequences (automated drip campaigns), use templates, and share scheduling links — all through your connected Gmail account.


## What You Can Do

**Sequences (Drip Campaigns)**
- **Browse** your sequences and see recipient stats (enrolled, finished, bounced)
- **Review** sequence stages — subject lines, content, delays, and send windows
- **Enroll** contacts into sequences with personalised template variables

**Email**
- **Send** tracked emails through Gmail with automatic open and click tracking
- **View** sent, draft, and scheduled emails with engagement data (opens, clicks, timestamps)

**Templates (Snippets)**
- **Browse** your personal and shared email templates
- **Send** templates to recipients with personalised variables (e.g. name, company)

**Scheduling**
- **View** your meeting types (duration, location, booking link)
- **Share** scheduling links in emails or conversations


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **Mixmax** and click **Set up**
3. Click **Open Mixmax** to go to your Integrations settings
4. Scroll to the **API Key** section and click **Generate Token**
5. Copy the token — it won't be shown again
6. Paste it back in Rebel

> **Plan requirement**: Mixmax API access requires a **Growth or Enterprise annual plan**. Free and monthly plans don't include API access.


## Tips

- **Check engagement**: "Did Sarah open my last email?" — Rebel checks open and click tracking data
- **Enrol contacts in sequences**: "Add alice@acme.com and bob@acme.com to the Q1 Outreach sequence"
- **Use templates**: "Send the follow-up template to sarah@acme.com" — Rebel finds the template and fills in variables
- **Share scheduling links**: "What's my 30-minute meeting link?" — Rebel finds it so you can share it
