# Pipedrive

Access your Pipedrive CRM data: manage deals and pipelines, create and update contacts and organisations, log activities, and track your sales process.


## What You Can Do

- **Deals**: Create, update, search, and track deals through pipeline stages
- **Contacts**: Manage person records with custom fields, activities, and deal associations
- **Organisations**: Create and search companies, link to contacts and deals
- **Activities**: Schedule and track calls, meetings, tasks, and follow-ups
- **Pipelines**: View pipeline stages, deal distribution, and conversion stats
- **Search**: Find deals, contacts, and organisations across your Pipedrive data
- **Notes & files**: Attach notes and manage files on deals, contacts, and organisations


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **Pipedrive** and click **Set up**
3. Click **Open Pipedrive** to go to your API settings
4. Scroll to **Your personal API token**
5. Copy the token
6. Paste it back in Rebel

> **Note**: Each Pipedrive user has their own API token. The token grants access to all data visible to your account.


## Tips

- **Pipeline overview**: "What's in my sales pipeline?" or "Show deals by stage"
- **Deal management**: "Create a new deal for Acme Corp worth $25,000" or "Move the Acme deal to negotiation stage"
- **Contact lookup**: "Find all contacts at Acme Corporation and show their deals"
- **Activity tracking**: "Schedule a follow-up call with Jane at Acme for tomorrow" or "Show my overdue activities"
- **Reporting**: "How many deals did we close this month?" or "What's the total value of deals in qualification?"
