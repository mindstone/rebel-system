# Humaans

Access your Humaans HR platform: list employees, view your org chart, check job roles and departments, see who's on leave, and request time off.


## What You Can Do

- **List employees** and browse your team directory
- **View org chart** and reporting structures
- **Check job roles** and department assignments
- **See who's on leave** and upcoming time off
- **Request time off** through Rebel


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **Humaans** and click **Set up**
3. Enter your Humaans API key (found in your Humaans account settings)

> **API key**: Go to your [Humaans token settings](https://app.humaans.io/settings/home?tokens=1) and click **Generate new token**.


## Tips

- **Team lookup**: Ask "who's in the engineering team?" or "show me the org chart"
- **Leave planning**: "Who's off next week?" or "request Friday off"
- **New starter info**: "What department is [name] in?" or "who does [name] report to?"
