# TalentLMS

Access your TalentLMS learning platform: manage courses and users, enrol learners, track progress, view test results, and generate SSO links.


## What You Can Do

- **Courses**: List, create, and manage courses
- **Users**: List, create, and manage learners
- **Enrolments**: Enrol or unenrol users in courses
- **Progress**: Track learner completion and training status
- **Groups & branches**: View organisational structure
- **Tests & surveys**: Access test and survey results
- **SSO**: Generate single sign-on links for learners


## Setup

1. Open **Settings → Connectors**
2. Find **TalentLMS** and click **Set up**
3. Log in to your TalentLMS admin panel
4. Go to **Account & Settings → Security**
5. Enable API access and copy the API key
6. Enter your subdomain (e.g., "acme" for acme.talentlms.com) and API key in Rebel

> **Requirements**: Paid TalentLMS plan with Super Admin access. Rate limits vary by plan (2,000-10,000 calls/hour).


## Tips

- **Course overview**: "Show all active courses in TalentLMS"
- **Learner progress**: "Check Jane's training progress" or "Who hasn't completed Onboarding 101?"
- **Bulk enrolment**: "Enrol the sales team in the new product training course"
- **Results**: "Show test results for the compliance quiz"
