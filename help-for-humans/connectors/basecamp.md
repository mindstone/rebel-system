# Basecamp

Access your Basecamp projects: browse kanban boards, create and move cards, add comments, track checklist steps, and view team members.


## What You Can Do

- **Projects**: List and browse your Basecamp projects
- **Kanban boards**: View columns, cards, and their details
- **Cards**: Create tasks with checklists, update cards, move between columns
- **Comments**: Add comments to any item
- **People**: List team members in your account
- **Steps**: Mark checklist steps as complete or incomplete


## Setup

1. Go to [37signals Integrations](https://launchpad.37signals.com/integrations) and sign in
2. Click **Register another application**
3. Give it a name (e.g., "Rebel") and set the **Redirect URI** to `http://localhost:8787/callback`
4. Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**
5. Open **Settings → Connectors** in Rebel
6. Find **Basecamp** and click **Set up**
7. Paste the Client ID and Client Secret, then click **Connect**
8. Ask Rebel to "authenticate with Basecamp" — your browser will open to complete sign-in

> **Note**: You need a Basecamp account. After the initial sign-in, tokens refresh automatically.


## Tips

- **Browse projects**: "Show my Basecamp projects" or "What's on the board?"
- **Create tasks**: "Create a card for 'Design review' in the To Do column"
- **Move cards**: "Move the Design review card to In Progress"
- **Add comments**: "Add a comment to that card saying we're on track"
- **Check progress**: "Show me the enriched details for card X" (includes comments and attachments)
