# Migrating from Other AI Tools

If you've been using ChatGPT or Claude.ai, you can bring your conversation history, memories, and custom configurations into Rebel.

## How It Works

Both OpenAI and Anthropic let you export your data. Once you have the export, ask Rebel to help you import it — Rebel will walk you through extracting the useful parts and saving them to your memory.

## From ChatGPT

1. Export your data from [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) → Settings → Data Controls → Export
2. Download and unzip the file when you receive the email
3. Tell Rebel: **"Help me import my ChatGPT history"** and provide the folder path

Rebel can extract your custom instructions, memories, conversation patterns, and even convert Custom GPT prompts into Rebel skills.

## From Claude.ai

1. Export your data from [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) → Settings → Privacy → Export Data
2. Download and unzip the file when you receive the email
3. Tell Rebel: **"Help me import my Claude.ai history"** and provide the folder path

Note: Claude.ai's memory feature and Projects must be copied separately — Rebel will guide you through this.
