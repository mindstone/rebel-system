# My Sources

A quiet place to find your meetings, notes, and the source material Rebel has already met. Useful when "where did we say that?" becomes a lifestyle.

## What it does

- Lists your recent sources — meetings, notes, recordings, imports
- Lets you open a source and read the summary, key takeaways, and related material
- Shows search and filtering for when you have more than a handful of sources

## When to use it

Before a meeting, when you want last week's notes in front of you. After a call, when you want to skim what was actually said. Any time the phrase "I know we talked about this somewhere" leaves your mouth.

## Data and control

This plugin reads sources that already live in your Rebel workspace — meetings you've recorded, notes you've imported, transcripts Rebel has produced. It doesn't fetch anything from the internet and doesn't share anything with anyone. You can turn it off, edit its source, fork it, or delete your copy from the Library lens at any time.

## For tinkerers

This started life as a Rebel built-in plugin. Your editable copy lives in `Chief-of-Staff/plugins/sources-browser/`. Open `index.tsx` to see how it talks to the workspace, change what it shows, or build something else off the same scaffolding. You're not breaking anything — your copy is yours.
